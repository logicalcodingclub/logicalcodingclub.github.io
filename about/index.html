About Us

Logical Coding club

What we do?

Logical Coding Club (LCC) provides programming training classes to Children aged 12+. We are based in the beautiful City of York. At present, we only provide online training courses.

Our courses

We mainly provide programming courses with Python across different levels: beginners, intermediate, advanced and topic based classes. We also provided training classes for Java and Ruby.

Fees

  • £40 per hour for 1 to 1 sessions
  • £25 per student per hour for 1 to 2 sessions
  • £20 per student per hour for 1 to 3 sessions
